My Story

Welcome!

Thank you so much for checking out my work. My name is Gopika; I am a research professional who graduated from UC Irvine with a B.A. in Psychology and a minor in Biology. I have worked at several research labs over the years: the Sleep and Cognition Lab, the Learning and Neuroscience Decision Lab, and University Neurocognitive Imaging. Each lab focused on research in a different area within psychology and neuroscience; I really got to see how people think, understand, and function. I started investigating interesting careers in Psychology and stumbled upon CareerFoundry’s UX Design course. It was life-changing, to say the least. Here, I tackled my very own project (check it out in my Work section!) and grew passionate about UX Research. It felt like I was combining my love for research, art, and psychology all into one. After attending a few conferences and watching how innovative, brilliant, and friendly everyone was, I fell in love with the design community as well. These inspired me to pursue the field. I have mastered a wide array of soft and technical skills within research and psychology and want to apply what I have learned as a UX Researcher one day. Please don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any feedback, comments, or if you would like to work together!
